About The Sky Imperial

Who We Are

"We didn't just want to build hotels. We wanted to build homes away from home — places where every guest feels they truly belong."

2015
Founded
The Beginning

A Vision Born
From Belonging

The Sky Imperial Hotels & Resorts was founded with a clear purpose: to create hospitality experiences that feel genuinely personal. Starting with our flagship Bapu's Resort in Dwarka, Gujarat, we set out to combine the warmth of Indian hospitality with world-class amenities.

Today, with 25+ properties across three states and a 4.3★ Google rating from over 2,100 guests, that original vision has only grown stronger.

Our Growth

From One Resort
To 25+ Destinations

What began as a single beachfront resort has grown into one of India's fastest-expanding hospitality brands. We now serve business travellers, honeymooners, families, and event planners across Gujarat, Rajasthan, and Madhya Pradesh.

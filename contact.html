Policies & Info

Good to Know
Before You Arrive

🏨
Check-In / Check-Out

Standard check-in at 2 PM, checkout at 11 AM. Early check-in and late checkout available on request (subject to availability). Corporate package includes early check-in.

🚫
Cancellation Policy

Free cancellation up to 48 hours before arrival. Cancellations within 48 hours are charged 1 night's rate. Non-refundable rates offer the best pricing.

🐾
Pets & Visitors

Pet-friendly rooms available at select properties — please mention at time of booking. Day visitors welcome with prior arrangement.

🍼
Children Policy

Children under 5 stay free. Ages 6–12 are charged at ₹500/night for an extra bed with breakfast. Baby cots available on request at no charge.

💳
Payments Accepted

All major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and cash accepted. GST-compliant invoices available for corporate bookings.

🔒
Privacy & Security

Your personal data is encrypted and never shared with third parties. Booking data is stored securely in compliance with Indian data protection regulations.

FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

Is breakfast included in all rooms? +
Breakfast is included in select packages such as the Weekend Escape and Corporate Stay. Room-only rates do not include breakfast, but our restaurants are open for all meals with excellent value options.
How do I get the best rate? +
Always book directly with us at theskyimperial.com or by calling +91 95107 71506. Direct bookings consistently offer the lowest rates, flexible cancellation, and complimentary upgrades when available.
Do you offer airport transfers? +
Yes! Airport, railway station, and bus stand transfers can be arranged for all our properties. Please inform us at least 6 hours before arrival with your travel details and we'll coordinate pick-up.
Can I modify my booking after confirmation? +
Absolutely. Most bookings can be modified free of charge up to 48 hours before check-in. Simply call +91 95107 71506 or email bookings@theskyimperial.com with your booking reference.
Is WiFi available at all properties? +
Yes — high-speed complimentary WiFi is available throughout all Sky Imperial properties, including rooms, lobbies, restaurants, and meeting rooms. Corporate guests receive dedicated bandwidth allocation.
Do you host destination weddings? +
Yes, weddings are one of our specialities! We host ceremonies ranging from 50 to 800 guests across our properties. Our dedicated wedding coordinator will handle everything from décor to catering. Contact us early to check availability for your date.
